Unicost Investment Limited

Unicost Investment Limited is a design and build consortium based in Nairobi, Kenya. We specialize in offering comprehensive construction solutions—from concept design to project completion—ensuring seamless delivery, cost efficiency, and exceptional results.

With a dedicated in-house team of architects, engineers, quantity surveyors, and interior designers, combined with a skilled construction department, we provide an end-to-end service that eliminates the hassle of working with multiple consultants and contractors. This integrated approach allows us to maintain the highest standards of quality, safety, and efficiency in every project.

Our company is registered with the National Construction Authority (NCA) and holds valid licenses for Building Works and Road Works, a reflection of our professionalism and compliance with industry standards.





Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide clients with end-to-end design and construction services through a collaborative approach, combining creativity, technical expertise, and quality workmanship. With our registered NCA licenses, we strive to deliver excellence in residential, commercial, and infrastructure projects, ensuring safety, efficiency, and value for every client.



Vision Statement

To be a trusted leader in innovative design and construction solutions, delivering sustainable, functional, and timeless spaces that improve the quality of life and inspire future generations.
