Why We Were Founded

Unicost Investment Limited was founded to bridge the gap between design and construction by offering clients a one-stop solution for all their building needs. Many clients often face challenges dealing with multiple consultants and contractors, which can lead to delays, cost overruns, and compromised quality.

Our Team





Samuel Sitandi

Quantity Surveyor & Project Manager

Samuel Sitandi is the founder and principal of Unicost Investment Limited. With a deep passion for design and construction, he has dedicated his career to creating functional, sustainable, and aesthetically inspiring spaces.

Trained as a Quantity Surveyor and experienced in project management, Samuel brings a wealth of knowledge in both cost planning and project execution. His expertise spans residential, commercial, and infrastructure projects, where he combines technical precision with innovative solutions to deliver exceptional results.

As a leader, he believes in a collaborative approach—working closely with clients, consultants, and construction teams to ensure every project reflects the client’s vision while meeting the highest standards of quality, safety, and cost efficiency.

Under his guidance, Unicost Investment Limited has grown into a trusted design-and-build consortium, recognized for its professionalism, innovation, and commitment to excellence. Samuel continues to drive the company forward with a focus on integrity, client satisfaction, and sustainable development in Kenya’s built environment.
